Every commercial email must include a physical mailing address. This protects
your home address. A P.O. Box is perfectly legal.

OPTION A — USPS P.O. Box (cheapest):

OPTION B — Virtual Mailbox (no driving, digital scans):
  1. Go to: https://www.anytimemailbox.com
  2. Choose a New York address location
  3. Sign up (~$10-$15/month) — they scan all mail to your email

  2. Click your project (ellie-landing or similar)

  5. Name:  BUSINESS_MAILING_ADDRESS
     Value: The Style Refresh · PO Box XXXX · New York, NY XXXXX

  3. Go to Vercel → Your project → Settings → Environment Variables → Production

STEP 4 — SET UP SALES TAX (New York State + Stripe Tax)
  TIME: 25 minutes   COST: Free registration
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

New York taxes digital subscriptions. Stripe Tax handles collection automatically.

A) REGISTER FOR NY SALES TAX PERMIT (FREE):
  1. Go to: https://www.tax.ny.gov/bus/st/stidx.htm
  2. Click "Register for Sales Tax"
  3. Log in or create an NY.gov account
  4. Complete the Business Registration form:
     - Entity type: Sole proprietor
     - Business activity: Digital information services
     - Address: Your P.O. Box
  5. You'll receive your Certificate of Authority (NY vendor number) by mail
     Keep this — you need it to file your quarterly returns

B) ENABLE STRIPE TAX (AUTOMATIC COLLECTION):
  1. Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com/tax
  2. Click "Get started" → "Activate Stripe Tax"
  3. Add your NY registration number once you receive it
  4. Stripe will automatically:
     - Calculate the correct tax rate for each subscriber
     - Add it to their checkout bill
     - Track what you owe each quarter

C) QUARTERLY FILING (every 3 months):
  - Go to: https://www.tax.ny.gov → File your quarterly return
  - Stripe Tax shows you exactly how much to remit
  - Due dates: March 20 / June 20 / September 20 / December 20

HOW THE BUSINESS RUNS AUTOMATICALLY — YOUR WEEKLY ROUTINE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

THE SYSTEM DOES THIS WITHOUT YOU:
  Every Sunday 6:00 PM ET  → AI scrapes fashion trends (Vogue, Net-a-Porter, etc.)
                              Claude AI curates 3 looks (Executive / Weekender / Wildcard)
                              Saves the draft
                              Sends you an email with the 3 looks to review + Approve button

  Every Monday 7:00 AM ET  → IF you approved: sends the brief to all paying members
                              IF you didn't approve: sends you a warning email

WHAT YOU DO EVERY WEEK (THE ONLY THING):
  □ Sunday, anytime before midnight:
    Open the email from Ellie Curator (subject: "Sunday Draft — Week of...")
    Read the 3 looks. Like them? Click the BIG APPROVE BUTTON.
    That's it. Monday morning it sends automatically.

  □ If you don't like a look this week:
    Ignore the email. No send happens. You get a warning Monday morning.
    (In the future, we can add an "Edit" feature — let me know.)

WHAT YOU CHECK WEEKLY (10 MINUTES):
  □ Monday morning: Check that the brief sent successfully
    Go to: https://resend.com/emails → confirm emails delivered
  □ Monday morning: Check Stripe for new/cancelled subscribers
    Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com/subscriptions
  □ Any day: Check if anyone emailed hello@[yourdomain.com] with questions

THAT IS ALL. The business is otherwise fully automated.

THE EMAILS YOU'LL RECEIVE AND WHAT THEY MEAN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

EMAIL → Subject: "Sunday Draft — Week of [date]"
  Meaning: Your weekly brief is ready to review. Contains 3 looks + Approve button.
  What to do: Read it. If you like it, click Approve. If not, ignore it.

EMAIL → Subject: "[ACTION NEEDED] No approved draft — Monday send skipped"
  Meaning: Monday arrived and no approval was found. Members did NOT receive a brief.
  What to do: Either approve manually (click the link in the email) or skip this week.
  Prevention: Always approve by Sunday 11:59 PM ET.

EMAIL → Subject: "New Waitlist Application — [Name]"
  Meaning: Someone joined the waitlist. 
  What to do: Nothing required now. When you're ready, email them to subscribe.

EMAIL → Subject: "New Style Refresh Member — [Name]"
  Meaning: Someone just paid $19 and subscribed!
  What to do: Nothing — they get a welcome email automatically. Check Stripe to see revenue.

EMAIL → Subject: "Your Monday Style Brief — Week of [date]"
  Meaning: This is a COPY of what your members received. It goes to RESEND_NOTIFY_EMAIL.
  What to do: Read it to make sure it looks right. Archive it.

STEP 8 — SET UP AFFILIATE COMMISSIONS (Earn on every purchase your members make)
  TIME: 30 minutes   COST: Free to join   EARNS: 5–12% on every click that buys
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Every time a member clicks "Shop now →" in the Monday brief, you have a chance
to earn a commission on what they buy. The system is already built — you just
need to sign up and add one environment variable.

HOW IT WORKS:
  - Every buy link in the member email goes through your site (/api/go)
  - That route redirects through Sovrn Commerce (formerly VigLink)
  - Sovrn automatically recognizes 50,000+ retailers including Net-a-Porter,
    Nordstrom, Farfetch, SSENSE, Revolve, Shopbop, Saks, Bloomingdale's, etc.
  - If a member buys anything within 30 days, you earn 5–12% commission
  - Sovrn pays out monthly via PayPal or ACH once you reach $10

PART A — Sign up for Sovrn Commerce:
  1. Go to: https://www.sovrncommerce.com
  2. Click "Get Started" → choose "Publisher" (that's you)
  3. Enter your site URL: https://stylebyellie.com
  4. Apply — approval is usually instant or within 24 hours
  5. After approval: click your name (top right) → "Account Settings"
  6. Copy your "Publisher Key" (a long string of letters/numbers)

PART B — Add your key to Vercel:
  1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard → your project → Settings → Env Variables
  2. Add new variable (PRODUCTION only):
       Name:  SOVRN_PUBLISHER_KEY
       Value: [paste your Publisher Key from step 6 above]
  3. Save, then Redeploy
  That's it. Every email link now earns you commission automatically.

PART C — Set up Skimlinks for the website (optional but adds more revenue):
  Skimlinks monetizes every retail link on your website (the blog pages, etc.)
  1. Go to: https://skimlinks.com → Apply as a publisher
  2. Enter your site URL and wait for approval (usually 1–2 business days)
  3. After approval: Dashboard → "Tools" → find your Publisher ID (a number)
  4. Go to Vercel → Settings → Env Variables → Add:
       Name:  SKIMLINKS_PUBLISHER_ID
       Value: [your Publisher ID number]
  5. Redeploy — every outbound retail link on the site is now monetized

WHAT TO EXPECT:
  - You won't get rich from this immediately — at 20 members, maybe $5–$40/month
  - At 200 members who actually click and buy: $500–$1,500/month in commissions
  - It costs you nothing and requires zero ongoing work — it's fully automated
